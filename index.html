History of Nordic Minimalism

Nordic Minimalism emerged through decades of Scandinavian design philosophy, emphasizing clarity, natural light, honest materials, and functional simplicity. Over the 20th century, influences like functionalism, democratic design, and environmental awareness shaped the style into the calm, structured aesthetic recognized today.
